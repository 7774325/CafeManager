## 2. POS LOGIC ‚Äì ‚ÄúLOYVERSE+, BUT BETTER‚Äù

### Payment & Billing Rules (Very Important)

* **No artificial limits**:

  * Payment methods
  * Number of tables
  * Products
  * Outlets
* Support:

  * Cash
  * Card
  * Wallets
  * Split payments
  * Room + F&B combined bills

### Credit Billing (Key Clarification)

CKMS **must support credit billing**, unlike Loyverse.

**Professional definition of expected behavior:**

* Trusted customers may be allowed to purchase on credit.
* When an item is saved as a **credit sale**:

  * Inventory must be deducted immediately.
  * The sale is recorded as *credited*, not *pending*.
* During **daily shift close / settlement**:

  * There must be **zero pending bills** in the billing screen.
  * Credit sales are treated as *account receivables*, not open transactions.
* Settlement logic:

  * Cash/card totals must reconcile exactly.
  * Credit totals are logged separately for reporting and customer account tracking.
* No unpaid or hanging bills are allowed to block shift closing.

This must be reflected clearly in:

* POS behavior
* Reports
* Inventory movement
* Financial summaries
